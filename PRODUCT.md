# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Cat Deal Radar is a single-user tool for a cat owner with two adult cats. The
user checks it during work or daily routines to quickly decide whether a newly
found Douban cat-product deal is worth opening, saving, or dismissing.

## Product Purpose

The product monitors Douban cat deal posts, filters and scores relevant cat
food, wet food, freeze-dried treats, and litter deals, then presents timely
recommendations with simple feedback actions. Success means the user does not
miss limited-time good deals and can make a purchase decision in seconds.

## Brand Personality

Cute, light, and lived-in. The interface should feel personal and warm without
becoming childish, noisy, or promotional. It should read like a practical daily
radar made for one cat household, not a generic shopping product.

## Anti-references

Avoid ecommerce promotion pages, flashy dashboards, and corporate admin panels.
Do not use aggressive sale badges, heavy KPI widgets, dense enterprise chrome,
or decorative effects that make the deal list harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the deal, not the system.
- Make confidence and priority instantly scannable.
- Keep feedback actions easy to tap and emotionally clear.
- Add personality through small details, not clutter.
- Prefer calm motion that confirms updates and interaction state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, strong keyboard focus states, mobile-first touch
targets, and reduced-motion support. Motion is welcome when it is lightweight,
stateful, and safe to disable for users who prefer reduced motion.
